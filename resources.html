Apps, tools and more for your business

Tools, apps, software are a necessity for any business, but even more so for online businesses. With the plethora of tools on the market today, and growing daily, it can be a chore to choose the tools we want and need to work with.

Tools & Apps

Honeybook

HoneyBook is a comprehensive client management platform designed for independent businesses and small business owners. It offers a range of features to streamline various aspects of running a business, particularly those involving client interactions and management.

Asana

Asana is a software-as-a-service platform that focuses on team collaboration and work management. It allows teams to create projects, assign tasks, set deadlines, and communicate directly within the platform.

Clickup

Clickup is a comprehensive project management tool designed to cater to various businesses and team sizes across different industries. It offers a collaborative environment for teams to plan, track progress, and manage projects effectively in the cloud.

Canva

Need stunning images for your website & social media but don’t have design skills? Canva is awesome for that reason, it comes with tons of templates too to make it even easier.
